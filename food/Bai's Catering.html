Good to Know

1. On-site service only. We come to you — please have an outdoor space equivalent to two parking spots available.

2. Charcoal-fired roasting with minimal smoke. The equipment is compact and presentable.

3. Roasting takes 4–5 hours. Please let us know your desired dining time when booking.

4. Only one lamb per day. Early booking with a deposit is required to secure your date.
